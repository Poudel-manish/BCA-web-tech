Bachelor in Computer Application(BCA)

Bachelorin computer Application(BCA) is a four year degree run by TU,Nepal. The main focus of the coures is towards the application development. Student can seek their career as a IT professional after the completion of the course. Apart from that syudent can also work in different area like design and multimedia, network security, system administration and so on.

Bachelor in Hotel Management(BHM)

This course cover the fundamental of hotel ,food and Beverage production , hospitality, office and housekeeping along with familiarizaing the student with the multi dimention of hospitality industry.
BHM course is both theoretical and practical in nature. From this this is the one of the field whose scope is growing extensively day by day. BHM is very dazzing, colourfull and challenging career.

Bachelor in Social Work(BSW)

This course is degined with the purpose of giving theoritical and conceptual based knowledge to the student. To build up qualified and dedicated human resourse. BSW is the leading faculty in terms of number of subject offered and the number of department it have. BSW is the course offered by TU which operate most of the social sector of the nation.

Bachelor in Business Studies(BBS)

BBS is the four year degree program conducted by TU and FOM. It is an annual exam based program. The main objective of BBS is to develop student into dynamic manager having ability to handle responsibility in every sector.

About Us

Itahari Namuna College, a pioneer education institution in Itahari, was established in 2056 BS with the aim at producing proactive graduates. It has been providing world class education to meet the demand of present world. The institution is led by a team of visionary and dedicated academic professionals. Itahari Namuna College runs classes from beginner to bachelor level under the same management.

Moreover, the college has ultra-modern laboratories, well stocked library and well ventilated modern buildings with spacious classrooms. It is well known for its sound academic environment, qualified teaching faculty, innovative teaching pedagogies, transparent and scientific evaluation mechanism with modern and persistently updated new and professional streams of education. It has brought about myriads of academic reforms in Province-1. It is the pioneer college in offering a syllabus of Social Work, and thereby popularly recognized as a social work college in the eastern region. The college is fully dedicated to internalizing self-confidence, self-discipline and ethic in our products.

The college has been imparting quality and project-based education to meet the needs of the changing world. It provides various scholarship schemes to deserving candidates. It has been successful in obtaining hundred percent results since its inception. In this regard, the legacy has been kept uncharred till date. In addition, the college has also developed itself to be the best academic institution in BSW in the eastern region in terms of its results and its students’ performance. Similarly, the college has stood second and third in the national level results. Besides, it is the first college to offer BHM program in the eastern region
